2026 Venture / Tooling

Thesis App

A workbench for drafting and pressure-testing investment theses.

A structured tool for moving an investment idea from gut to thesis — capturing market, team, competitive dynamics, and assumptions in one place. Built from the venture fellowship habit of having to say, out loud, why this company and why now.

2025 Side Project

Cinder

A small, focused web app — built end-to-end as an exercise in shipping.

A lightweight personal product built as a study in scope: pick one thing, ship it, learn from it. The kind of side project that exists less to solve a market and more to keep the muscle of building from atrophying during a busy semester.

2022 Game / JS

Platformer

A browser game built to study how interaction actually feels.

A side-scrolling platformer written in JavaScript — physics, collisions, input timing, all of it from scratch. Built less to make a game and more as a reminder that product quality lives in the small loops: how a jump lands, how a button responds, how friction shapes attention.
